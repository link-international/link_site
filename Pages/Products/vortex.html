Rear Sprockets

For road-going and road racing motorcycles, Vortex racing has a complete range of rear sprockets, again made in the USA using the highest quality aluminium

7075 T6 grade alloy is used to manufacture the supersport and superbike rears. Less rotating weight improves acceleration and allows the suspension to do its job correctly. Available in hard coating PTFE black for maximum strength and durability and in anodised red, black and blue.

CNC machined to the tightest tolerances.

Vortex's unique manufacturing process creates a perfectly concentric sprocket every time, which frees horsepower and extends the life of both chain and sprocket. The perfected tooth design has less drag and reduces friction.

Attractive, innovative design.

Available for almost every off-road bike made in anodised black hard coat with PTFE for extraordinary life or in red, black or blue anodised with machined mud grooves giving a unique, Vortex-patented look.

CNC machined brake and clutch levers

Anodised in black, blue, gold and red, Vortex levers come in standard and "shorty" designs. Multi-adjustable and very strong, these levers improve the feel and performance of your motorcycle, and they are available for most modern sportsbikes.

Vortex Rearsets

Manufactured for most modern sportsbikes from high tensile aluminium and CNC machined for a perfect fit, these rearsets feature 12-way adjustability, folding toe pegs, dual-bearing linkages and Vortex's patented knurl-design footpegs for maximum control in all situations.
