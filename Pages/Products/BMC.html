BMC have more than 30 years experience in the motorcycling industry from which to draw their experience in producing an air filter unmatched in quality and performance.

BMC Airfilter Range

DESIGNED FOR HIGH PERFORMANCE

BMC air filters are designed to ensure a substantially higher air flow than original paper filters. BMC air filters are made from cotton to reduce the loss of air flow pressure passing through the air filter and through this method are able to create the best conditions for maximum power. The benefits of replacing your original paper filter with a BMC equivalent is that you will ensure your engine receives unrivalled clean air flow with substantial horsepower gains.

INDUSTRY LEADING EXPERTS

BMC has qualified engineers using advanced software and expert technicians using the latest technology to produce the world leading BMC filters. All BMC motorcycle filters utilize alloy mesh with epoxy coating to ensure long lasting protection from petrol fumes and oxidation from humidity. The filtering material is composed of special cotton gauze soaked with ultra low viscosity oil to give 0unmatched air permeability.
