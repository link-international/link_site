The result is an unequalled presentation of motorcycle footwear: multiple collections each with a range in style, function and value to offer THE IDEAL BOOT FOR EVERY RIDER. These ensure that riders can keep their boots in pristine condition for years to come

TCX Boots Catalogue

DOUBLE FLEX CONTROL SYSTEM

The D.F.C. System features a PU frame attached to the boot with two lateral hidden screws positioned on the heel counter, at ankle level. At the back of the boot, the system is equipped with two lugs that slide inside dedicated ‘safety lock’ pockets which allow a back flexibility up to 13 degrees, avoiding the overextension of the ankle joint to limit the risk of injury.

FASTEN FIT CONTROL (LACING SYSTEM)

The internal fastening system features long-lasting laces joined to the boot lining and a newly designed padded tongue to offer an increased level of comfort. It ensures that the upper wraps the foot, providing maximum fit precision and sensitivity while riding. The Fasten Fit Control offers an adjustable fit according to the rider’s individual foot anatomy.

NEW MICHELIN® BURNOUT SOLE

Our new Michelin Burnout Sole technology helps to add control, stability, tear and abrasion resistance as well as superior durability.
