CAUTION

  • For external charging use the Motocell Lithium Gold Battery Charger suitable for LiFePO4 series.
  • Do not disassemble or modify the battery.
  • Avoid excessive physical shock.
  • Do not use or store battery near fire or heat.
  • Store in a cool dry surroundings.
  • When required, dispose to Eco Friendly Recycling Depot.
