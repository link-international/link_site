CAUTION

  • Only use a dedicated Lithium Battery Charger suitable for LiFePO4 series.
  • Do not press charge test indicator button longer than 3 seconds.
  • Do not disassemble or modify the battery in any way.
  • Do not strike, throw or subject the battery to physical shock.
  • Do not use or store battery near fire or heat.
  • Store in a cool dry surroundings.
  • When battery exhausted, dispose to Recycling Depot.
