States MX

States MX is an innovative brand that was first established in 2012, owned and trademarked exclusively by Link International. States MX came together with an aim to bring riders of all off-road motorcycles quality products that would improve the look and the reliability of their bikes at an affordable price.

States MX Catalogue

QUALITY ASSURED

States MX products are the result of constant and careful analysis paying particular attention to the present and future needs of all types of race and recreational motorcycle enthusiasts. Using the latest computer designed and controlled metal cutting equipment, the stringent controls of the R&D Engineers and specialized technicians, States MX ensures high quality production that keeps it at the market forefront.

EXTENSIVE RANGE

The extensive range of off-road parts from States MX includes the Fusion steel/alloy combination sprockets, complete wheels, bar ends, case savers, foot pegs, kickstart levers, rims, hour meters, launch controls, brake levers, clutch lever and perch assemblies, fuel hoses, brand specific bolt kits and much more. All these products and much more, all at great prices.
