AGV’s history is a story of racers, enthusiasts and passion. The company that produced the world’s first fiberglass helmet in 1954 has been linked to many riders who have made motorcycle history, including Giacomo Agostini, Angel Nieto, Barry Sheene and Wayne Rainey up to the present day with Valentino Rossi.

AGV’s helmet production process has revolutionized helmet design in a way that is demonstrable and measurable in terms of protection, ergonomics and comfort. AGV helmets are undoubtedly born out of a passion for racing that is second to none.

AGV offer a full range of full face, open face, flip front and off-road helmets for everyone's biking needs.
