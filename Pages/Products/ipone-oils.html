IPONE Oils

Since 1985, Ipone set out to produce lubricants for mopeds and scooters and were initially dedicated to two-stroke engines. We branched out into the off-road motorcycle sector and eventually grew to include four-stroke bikes and recreational vehicles like quads and jet skis. Soon we became popular with the younger motorcycle users due to our dynamic and exciting product range and association with younger generation sporting events and stars.

Ipone now exclusively produces ultra-high quality performance focused oils, targeted specifically at the powersport market. We also have a large range of motorcycle maintenance and care products and even feature some quirky products like our strawberry scented two-stroke oils!

Ipone is a big hitter in the racing scene; we have sponsored the MXGP, MXoN and SNX since 2014. We are the exclusive and official supplier of the Red Bull MotoGP Rookies Cup and the development of Ipone’s flagship oil, Stroke 4, was made in close collaboration with their technical team.

For the most complete range of lubricants on the market dedicated to motorbikes, scooters and recreational vehicles, it’s got to be Ipone.
