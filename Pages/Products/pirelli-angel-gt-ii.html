ANGEL GT II FEATURES

 

ANGEL™ GT II features dual-compound on rear, and single compound on the front tyres. According to PIRELLI know-how in the sport-touring segment, this compound scheme represents the expedient to achieve all sport-touring target performances in all different conditions, which includes durability, even wear and stability when loaded.

ANGEL™ GT II rears feature a dual-compound layout with cap-and-base scheme.

ANGEL™ GT II delivers outstanding grip and confidence in wet conditions thanks to the high-silica compound level through to a tread pattern derived from racing DIABLO™ WET and RAIN technologies to ensure mechanical grip and water drainage.
