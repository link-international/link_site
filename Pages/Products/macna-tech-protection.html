FINGER PROTECTION

The height of the knuckle protectors on the fingers make your gloves slide without your knuckles touching the ground. The open bridge construction keeps material to a minimum and protects the optional ventilation opening.

 

R.I.S.C. PALM PROTECTION

An external hard shell part, works as a sliding pad or spreads the impact.It especially protects the pisciform bone and it’s surroundings.

 

HEATSHIELD

Heat resistant at the bottom.These Heatshields will prevent your pant from a melt down when accidentally touching your bike’s hot engine or exhaust pipe.

 

30 TPR PROTECTION

TPR rubber parts are injected directly to the fabric.
These thick rubber parts on the knuckles bring in extra protection, yet are flexible enough to remain comfortable.
