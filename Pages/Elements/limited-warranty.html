Limited Warranty Policies

Link International Pty Ltd offers this “Limited Warranty” on parts or products, supplied by us, which the following conditions are part thereof.

This Limited Warranty will only cover parts or products which have been directly imported by Link International Pty Ltd.

This Limited Warranty will only cover parts and products against a Manufacturing Defect.

This Limited Warranty may cover certain parts and products on a PRO-RATA basis only.

This Limited Warranty DOES NOT COVER any other aspect of a Claim, other than that of the defective part/s or product/s.

This Limited Warranty DOES NOT COVER any parts or products which have been subject to misuse, modification, negligence, accidental damage, incorrect maintenance, incorrect fitment, improper storage, or high-pressure cleaners / steam cleaners.

This Limited Warranty DOES NOT COVER certain machine type parts or products fitted by the public, which we deem must be fitted by a Registered Motorcycle Workshop.

This Limited Warranty DOES NOT COVER any parts or products supplied by Link International which are fitted to any type of Competition Vehicle or vehicle used on any Track or Test Day.

Dealers and Consumers Please Read This Important Acknowledgement:

No person/s acting on or behalf of Link International Pty Ltd, have the authorization to make a Warranty Claim decision, prior to the claimed part/s or product/s being returned to Link International Pty Ltd, for their inspection.

Dealers, please follow this procedure for making a WARRANTY CLAIM.

Where possible, Dealer is to inspect the defective part or product whilst still fitted to the vehicle, to determine correct fitment and regular maintenance has been carried out.

Contact Link International Pty Ltd before proceeding with this Warranty Claim.

Dealer is to provide Link International Pty Ltd a copy of Workshop Job Card/s and/or customer purchase Receipt.

Dealer is to return the completed Request Form, including the claimed part/s or product/s and all receipts to Link International P/L.

Note:

Link International Pty Ltd, will determine by using the information supplied on the “Warranty against a Manufacturing Fault” Form and by inspecting the claimed part/s whether a legitimate claim is warranted. (Further information may be requested)

Some Part/s may require further Testing and/or Analyses, to determine the exact cause of failure, this may extend the time period before a final decision can be made.

Once an outcome has been determined, Link International will contact the claiming Dealer.
