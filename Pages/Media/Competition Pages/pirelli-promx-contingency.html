ProMX Pirelli Moto Contingency Program

$15 000 in Contingency is up for grabs across three classes to privateer racers in 2023.

Welcome to Pirelli Australia’s contingency program for the 2023 Australian ProMX Championships. For 2023, privateer riders racing in the Australian ProMX Championship and committing to racing on Pirelli MX32 tyres will be eligible to be paid contingency dollars every round, with an additional overall Championship bonus to be paid at the end of the season.

This contingency extends across the four ProMX Championship classes, MX1, MX2, MX3 & MXW and is open to all privateer riders. Please read the following carefully to see if you qualify, and for more info on the contingency and terms and conditions.

Pirelli’s ProMX contingency is open to all privateer riders. Privateer status and eligibility will be determined upon receipt of the application.

PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED FOR THE WHOLE SEASON TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR THE CHAMPIONSHIP BONUS CONTINGENCY DOLLARS.

PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ROUND CONTINGENCY DOLLARS.

YOU MUST POST TO SOCIAL MEDIA AFTER EACH ROUND AND TAG @PIRELLIMOTOAUS IN EACH POST.

To qualify for contingency eligibility using Pirelli MX tyres, a Pirelli logo must be displayed on the bike's fork guards and rear guard, and Pirelli MX32 tyres must be used both on the front and rear of the motorcycle for the entirety of the race weekend.

Once your application is approved, we will send you a welcome pack with stickers and other promo items.

You do not necessarily have to be part of the official podium placings to receive contingency dollars. You only need to be placed in each classes overall Privateer podium rankings. If you are ranked in the top 3 overall privateer placings and are a registered Pirelli Moto athlete, you will receive the following contingency dollars per round:

MX1

  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

MX3

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

MXW

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

This is a contingency program on offer to all privateer riders competing in the Australian ProMX Championship. Riders can commit to racing one round, two rounds, or the entire season. For the privateers who do race the whole season, there is potentially a big pay day on offer, with a $1000 Championship bonus available to all classes. The highest overall ranked privateer in the MX1, MX2, MX3 & MXW classes, if registered and having raced on Pirelli MX32 tyres all season, will take home $1000. It’s that simple! Get the results, and get paid cash.

All contingency pay-outs will be deposited into the winner's bank accounts in the weeks following each event once Motorcycling Australia has confirmed official race results. Please fill out the form below with all the relevant details. We will be in touch to confirm your application.

Terms and Conditions

Pirelli Australia's Australian ProMX Contingency Program is open to all privateer racers in the MX1, MX2, MX3 & MXW Championship classes of the regular 2023 season of the Australian ProMX Championship. Upon application, privateer status will be determined by the Australian distributor of Pirelli Moto tyres, Link International. Link International has the right to refuse any applicant for any reason. PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED FOR THE WHOLE SEASON TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR SEASON CONTINGENCY. PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ROUND CONTINGENCY. YOU MUST POST TO SOCIAL MEDIA (INSTAGRAM ETC.) AFTER EACH ROUND AND TAG @PIRELLIMOTOAUS IN EACH POST. The Pirelli logo must be displayed on the bike's fork guards and rear guard. All contingency pay-outs will be deposited into the winner's bank accounts in the weeks following each event. Only registered and approved privateer riders competing in the 2023 ProMX Championship are eligible for this contingency program. Privateer finishing positions will be determined based on official race results, and all privateers will be ranked whether they have entered this contingency program or not. To be awarded prize money, you must be part of the overall Privateer podium rankings and be registered as a Pirelli Moto athlete.
