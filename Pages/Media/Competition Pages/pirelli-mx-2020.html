QLD Sunshine State MX Series & NSW State MX Series - 

Pirelli Tyre Contingency Programme 2020  

 

If you are competing in the SMX Series in Qld or the State MX Series in NSW in 2020 you have the chance of Winning a FREE SET of Pirelli Scorpion MX Tyres from each round. Each round will have one pair of Senior and one pair of Junior Pirelli Scorpion MX Tyres up for grabs. 

To to eligible to win you must have Pirelli tyres fitted to your bike for the series.

Simply complete the entry form below, attaching a photo of yourself and your bike fitted with Pirelli Tyres competing at one of the listed events and you will be in with a chance to win a free set of Pirelli MX Tyres.

This competition is not purely based on podium results, everyone has a chance to win.

*Term and Conditions

Entry must be lodged using the online form above, and must include a photo of your bike fitted with Pirelli Tyres at one of the listed events in 2020. All fields in the entry form must be completed for the entry to be valid. Each round will have one pair of Senior Pirelli Scorpion MX tyres and one pair of Junior Pirelli Scorpion MX tyres on offer. Only one entry per person per round will be valid. The winners will be contacted by email and their photo will be posted on Pirelli and Link International social media pages. Winners will be selected based on supplied race report and photo. The judges decision will be final and no correspondence will be entered into.
