Australian Superbike Championship Pirelli Privateer Tyre Contingency

 

Welcome to Pirelli Australia's contingency program for the 2023 Australian Superbike season. This contingency extends across the three ASBK championship classes; Superbike, Supersport and Supersport 300. It is open to all privateer riders across these three championship classes, with round-win/podium prizes and a cash prize for the series' highest points. Please read the following carefully to see if you qualify and for more info on the prizes and terms and conditions.

Pirelli Australia's ASBK contingency is only open to privateer riders. To be classified as a privateer, you must adhere to the following:

  • You are NOT a paid factory or private team rider.
  • You own the bike you race or have a distributor bike plan.
  • You do NOT receive any distributor sponsorship.
  • You pay for your racing consumables (tyres, oil etc.)

 

Pirelli Tyres must be used for the whole season to be eligible for Season prizes.
Pirelli Tyres must be used to be eligible for round prizes.

The Pirelli logo must be shown on the bike's front guard / fork legs and worn on the rider's leathers. Once approved, we will send you confirmation of your registration and welcome pack with stickers and other Pirelli promo items.

Terms and Conditions

Pirelli Australia (Link International)'s Australian Superbike Contingency Program (Pirelli Contingency) is open to all privateer racers in the Superbike, Supersport and Supersport 300 championship classes of the regular 2023 season of the Australian Superbike Championship (ASBK). Pirelli Australia deems that a privateer is a rider that is not factory supported in any way, owns their own bike or is on a distributor plan, does not receive distributor sponsorship and pays for their own race consumables. Pirelli Australia reserves the right to refuse any applicant for any reason. PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED FOR THE WHOLE SEASON TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR SEASON PRIZES. PIRELLI TYRES MUST BE USED TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ROUND PRIZES. Pirelli branding must feature on the bike's front guard/fork legs and the fairing sides, and a Pirelli patch/logo must be worn on the rider's leathers. Round winner tyre prizes are not redeemable for cash or other products and will be shipped to the winner's address in the weeks after the race. Pirelli Australia reserves the right to refuse the delivery of prizes for any reason.
