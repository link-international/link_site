ProMX Shot Racegear Contingency Program

$15 000 in Contingency is up for grabs across three classes to privateer racers in 2023.

Welcome to Shot Racegear Australia's contingency program for the 2023 Australian ProMX Championship. For 2023, privateer riders racing in the Australian ProMX Championship and committing to using Shot Racegear gear sets will be eligible to be paid contingency dollars every round, with an additional overall Championship bonus paid at the end of the season.

This contingency extends across the three ProMX Championship classes, MX1, MX2, MX3 & MXW and is open to all privateer riders. Please read the following carefully to see if you qualify, and for more info on the contingency and terms and conditions.

Shot Racegear’s ProMX contingency is open to all privateer riders. Privateer status and eligibility will be determined upon receipt of your application.

SHOT RACEGEAR GEAR SETS MUST BE USED FOR THE WHOLE SEASON TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR THE CHAMPIONSHIP BONUS CONTINGENCY DOLLARS.

SHOT RACEGEAR GEAR SETS MUST BE USED TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ROUND CONTINGENCY DOLLARS.

YOU MUST POST TO SOCIAL MEDIA AFTER EACH ROUND AND TAG @SHOTRACEGEAR_AUS IN EACH POST.

To qualify for contingency eligibility using Shot Racegear, the athlete must practice and race in a full set of Shot Racegear, which includes pants, jersey and gloves, for the entirety of the race weekend.

Once your application is approved, we will send you a welcome pack with stickers and other promo items.

You do not necessarily have to be part of the official podium placings to receive contingency dollars. You only need to be placed in each classes overall Privateer podium rankings. If you are ranked in the top 3 overall privateer placings and are a registered Shot athlete, you will receive the following contingency dollars per round:

MX1

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

MX2

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

MX3

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

MXW

  • 1st - $250
  • 2nd - $150
  • 3rd - $100

This is a contingency program on offer to all privateer riders competing in the Australian ProMX Championship. Riders can commit to racing one round, two rounds, or the entire season. For the privateers who do race the whole season, there is potentially a big pay day on offer, with a $1000 Championship bonus available to all classes. The highest overall ranked privateer in the MX1, MX2 and MX3 classes, if registered and having raced in Shot Racegear all season, will take home $1000. It’s that simple! Get the results, and get paid cash.

All contingency pay-outs will be deposited into the winner's bank accounts in the weeks following each event once Motorcycling Australia has confirmed official race results. Please fill out the form below with all the relevant details. We will be in touch to confirm your application.

Terms and Conditions

Shot Racegear Australia's Australian ProMX Contingency Program is open to all privateer racers in the MX1, MX2, MX3 & MXW Championship classes of the regular 2023 season of the Australian ProMX Championship. Upon application, privateer status will be determined by the Australian Shot Racegear distributor, Link International. Link International has the right to refuse any applicant for any reason. SHOT RACEGEAR GEAR SETS MUST BE USED FOR THE WHOLE SEASON TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR SEASON CONTINGENCY. SHOT RACEGEAR GEAR SETS MUST BE USED TO BE ELIGIBLE FOR ROUND CONTINGENCY. YOU MUST POST TO SOCIAL MEDIA (INSTAGRAM ETC.) AFTER EACH ROUND AND TAG @SHOTRACEGEAR_AUS IN EACH POST. All contingency pay-outs will be deposited into the winner's bank accounts in the weeks following each event. Only registered and approved privateer riders competing in the 2023 ProMX Championship are eligible for this contingency program. Privateer finishing positions will be determined based on official race results, and all privateers will be ranked whether they have entered this contingency program or not. To be awarded prize money, you must be part of the overall Privateer podium rankings and be registered as a Shot athlete.
